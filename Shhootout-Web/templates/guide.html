> YOUR MISSION

You have infiltrated in a secret enemy base and have got your hands on some key piece of information that can change the course of history.


Your task - is to make it safe and sound out of the building, while eliminating every enemy as silently as you can.

> HOW TO PLAY

To move around, use the WASD keys on your keyboard to move in a specific direction.

Use your mouse to aim, and shoot at enemies using your left-click.

> CREATING YOUR OWN SCENARIOS

Use your mouse to select what item to place in your scenario.

You may only place one room, and one player.

You can place as many enemies and items as your heart desires.

> SHARING YOUR CREATIONS

Save and share your playable levels with users from all around the world!

You can also load and play trending levels.

> PRO TIPS!

You lose life quickly! So be sure to not alert any surrounding enemies by shooting to nothing.

Your bullets are counted! Every shot made may make or break your mission.

Eliminating as many enemies and leaving as fast as possible, is sure to get your name on a level's leaderboard!
